    Services

    New product development

    Our commitment to supporting Food Business Operators (FBOs) extends beyond traditional solutions. We offer comprehensive R&D advisory services to assist FBOs in their quest for innovative product formulations. Our team of experts provides technical assistance, guiding FBOs through the process of developing new products from concept to reality. We understand the challenges involved in product formulation, which is why we offer specialized services tailored to the unique needs of each client. Whether it's creating a new recipe, enhancing an existing product, or addressing specific dietary requirements, we provide invaluable guidance and expertise throughout the development journey. Additionally, we offer literature survey services on behalf of FBOs, ensuring that they have access to the latest research, trends, and market insights. By conducting thorough surveys, we help FBOs stay informed and make well-informed decisions, saving them valuable time and effort. At Ashvamegha Solutions, our R&D advisory services aim to empower FBOs by bridging the gap between technical expertise and practical application. By leveraging our extensive knowledge and industry insights, we facilitate the development of cutting-edge products while ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. We are passionate about assisting FBOs in bringing their product ideas to life and propelling them towards success in the market. Our dedicated team is committed to delivering the highest quality R&D advisory services, enabling FBOs to thrive and stay ahead in the competitive food industry.

    Product Trials

    Once the product development process is complete and approved by the customer, we go a step further by helping in conducting product trials at the customer's location. Our goal is to ensure that the product meets the highest standards of quality and aligns with the customer's expectations. Our experienced team provides guidance and support throughout the product trial phase, working closely with the customer to establish the trial parameters and objectives. We assist in setting up the necessary protocols, monitoring the trial process, and collecting relevant data for evaluation. During the trial period, we remain committed to maintaining open lines of communication with the customer, addressing any concerns or issues that may arise. Our team conducts thorough assessments, analyzing the trial results to assess the product's performance, quality, and compliance with specified standards. By conducting product trials, we provide an opportunity to fine-tune and optimize the product before its full-scale launch. The trials allow for valuable insights and feedback, facilitating continuous improvement and ensuring that the final product meets or exceeds the desired expectations.






    Licensing and Registration

    We take pride in offering comprehensive assistance to Food Business Operators (FBOs) in obtaining the necessary licences and registrations from FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India). Our dedicated team provides full support throughout the application process, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience for our clients. We understand the intricacies and regulatory requirements involved in obtaining FSSAI licences or registrations. Our experts have in-depth knowledge of the application procedure and stay updated with the latest guidelines and norms. We guide FBOs through the entire process, from filling out the necessary forms to compiling the required documents. Our aim is to simplify the application process and minimize any potential roadblocks. We work diligently to ensure that FBOs meet the necessary criteria and submit accurate and complete applications. At Ashvamegha Solutions, we recognize the importance of FSSAI approvals for the smooth operation and credibility of FBOs. By offering full assistance and support, we enable our clients to navigate the regulatory landscape with confidence, ensuring compliance and facilitating their business growth. We are committed to delivering exceptional service and ensuring that our clients obtain the necessary licences or registrations to establish their food businesses in accordance with the standards set by FSSAI and the Government of India.

    Plant Layout

    We specialize in providing customized plant layout or production line layout solutions for our customers, tailored to their specific space availability and requirements. Our expert team ensures that the layout design optimizes industrial processes, adheres to international safety guidelines, and complies with relevant norms. Understanding the importance of an efficient and well-organized production facility, we meticulously analyze the available space and collaborate closely with our clients to understand their operational needs. Considering factors such as workflow, equipment placement, safety regulations, and future scalability, we design layouts that maximize productivity while ensuring a safe working environment. Our approach combines industry best practices with our knowledge of international safety standards and guidelines. We strive to create layouts that optimize resource utilization, minimize production bottlenecks, and promote smooth material flow. Our expertise enables us to design layouts that are ergonomically efficient, allowing for seamless operations and reducing the risk of accidents or hazards. By engaging our services, our customers can benefit from an optimized plant or production line layout that enhances efficiency, productivity, and safety. We are committed to delivering solutions that are customized to meet their unique needs and aspirations, ensuring their operational success in line with international standards.

    Label Verification

    We offer label verification services in accordance with FSSAI and Legal Metrology guidelines. Our experts meticulously analyze product labels, ensuring they meet all regulatory requirements. We verify critical information such as ingredients, nutritional values, allergen declarations, and other mandatory labeling elements. By leveraging our expertise and staying up to date with the latest regulations, we ensure that our client’s product labels are accurate, informative, and compliant. We help them navigate the complex landscape of regulatory requirements, mitigating the risk of non-compliance and potential legal consequences.










    PMFME Application Filling

    We are proud to be associated with the Government's PMFME (Prime Minister's Formalization of Micro Food Processing Enterprises) scheme, which aims to support MSMEs (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises) in the food processing sector. Through this collaboration, we assist MSMEs in Amritsar in accessing credit-based subsidies of up to 35%. Our team works closely with MSMEs, providing guidance and support throughout the process of availing the subsidy. We help them understand the eligibility criteria, documentation requirements, and the overall application process. With our expertise in the food processing industry and our knowledge of the PMFME scheme, we facilitate smooth and streamlined access to the available subsidies. By leveraging the PMFME scheme, MSMEs can obtain financial assistance to fuel their growth and expansion plans. This credit-based subsidy plays a vital role in providing the necessary support for investment in infrastructure, technology upgradation, and capacity building. At Ashvamegha Solutions, we are committed to empowering MSMEs and supporting their journey towards success. Through our association with the PMFME scheme, we strive to contribute to the growth and development of the food processing sector in Amritsar, facilitating access to much-needed financial support for MSMEs in the region.
