    About US

    Creating a safe food environment for consumers and healthy business environment for FBOs.

    Our solutions don't come out of a box ready to plug and play. We pride ourselves on identifying your challenges quickly and designing strategies to address them, always keeping top of mind your business's need for minimal disruption. Based out of Amritsar, Punjab we have an expertise in providing end to end solutions to Food Business Operators (FBOs).

    • We know the landscape, we know food market, and we understand food industry.
    • Our team has Food Technologists from various eminent institutes like Institute of Chemical Technology, Mumbai and Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar.
    • We help in getting approval for FSSAI registration/license which is required for any food-related business.
    • Our experts have developed products for many startups and few MNC's in food, beverage, nutraceuticals and health sector.
    Services

    Services

    These are some of the professional services which our company provides.

    New product development

    We provide the R&D advisory services advisory services in technical assistance to FBO for their new product formulation and also do literature survey on their behalf.

    Product Trials

    After developing and approving the product from the customer, we assist in conducting product trials at the customer's end in order to ensure the standards and quality of product.

    Licensing and Registration

    We provide full assistance to Food Business Operators in applying for FSSAI licence or registration and getting approved for the same from the Government of India.

    Plant Layout

    We provide the plant layout or the production line layout for the customers as per their space availability ensuring best industrial processes and following international safety guidelines and norms.

    Claim Validation

    Review the health & Nutritional claim based on scientific literature and provide the enough scientific literature to substantiate the claim.

    PMFME Application Filling

    We are also associated with governments's PMFME scheme to help MSME's in availing credit-based subsidy up to 35% in Amritsar.
