Mentor Program

Mentor Program

Be a part of the best mentorship program and gain invaluable experience

We believe in mentoring young food technologists to prepare them for various roles in food industry during their graduation and post-graduation years. During the tenure, the person will be mentored in food regulatory compliance, digital marketing, sales and project writing. Also, the person will be groomed in soft skills like time management, stress management, team management and others that are required to be successful in any field.


The working hours are flexible but we expect the person to dedicate at least a total of 8 working hours on weekdays and 4 working hours on Saturdays for us. 2-4 mentoring sessions are scheduled every month.


We believe in learning and growing together in the process and aims to work for the development of the organisation and team.


Session Openings: October & April
